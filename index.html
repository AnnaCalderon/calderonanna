Cluster and Sentiment Analyses in R

In this project, we applied cluster and sentiment analyses to American Airlines passenger reviews and satisfaction ratings. The data was obtained through surveys, and the goal was to identify key drivers of dissatisfaction. We used k-means clusters to inspect the characteristics of each segment based on (1) needs-based variables and (2) observable characteristics. Profiling clusters by needs-based variables helped us understand how the three market segments differ in their needs which in turn will aid the firm in offering products and services to match the segments' unique needs. Profiling clusters on observable characteristics helped identify customers to target them with the right solution to dissatisfaction.
